# Form

Schema-driven forms rendered with [JSON Forms](https://jsonforms.io/) and the `@ghentcdh/json-forms-vue` renderer set, extended with crouton-specific renderers (e.g. relations).

- **`form.vue`** — the form view rendered by the crouton router for create/edit operations.
- **Form modal** — open a resource form in a modal from anywhere.
- **Renderers** — the custom renderer entries crouton adds on top of `@ghentcdh/json-forms-vue`.
